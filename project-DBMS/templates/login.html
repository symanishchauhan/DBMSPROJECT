DYAL SINGH COLLEGE

Smart Lost & Found Management System

The Smart Lost & Found Management System is the official digital platform developed for Dyal Singh College to simplify the process of reporting and recovering lost items across the campus.

Students and staff can easily report lost belongings, search the system database for missing items, and submit claims for items that belong to them.

This platform provides a centralized, transparent, and efficient mechanism for managing lost property within the college premises, ensuring that valuable belongings are returned to their rightful owners quickly and securely.
